About

Lauren is a Philadelphia based astrologer, originally from Midtown Atlanta. She has 8+ years studying astrology, and has been reading professionally since 2012. Lauren loves how astrology enables her to better understand others, and in turn help people to better understand themselves. Empowerment is also a key theme. Lauren feels like - if you're not empowering your clients then wyd?


Lauren offers astrology and tarot readings via Skype, phone, FaceTime, and in person. She also has an astrology podcast, and YouTube channel.
